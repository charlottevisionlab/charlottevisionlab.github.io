Assistant Professor

Srijan Das

His research focuses on learning unified representations for long-form video by integrating multimodal inputs (e.g., language, 3D poses, and audio) across egocentric and exocentric viewpoints. Also, he explore vision–language–action models for robotics and diffusion-based video generation.

  • Video understanding
  • Robotic vision
  • Vision-language models
  • Action recognition
